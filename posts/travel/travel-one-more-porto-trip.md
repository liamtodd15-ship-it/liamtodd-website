The first Porto trip of the year, my 7th time visiting this city. Hopefully, I won't be 'visiting' for much longer, and I'll be settling down here.

This trip was nice for a few reasons. Firstly, we ticked off a lot of food places, that's always a plus. We went back to some old favourites, Fogo De Chao, RO Ramen, and obviously Casa Expresso. Then visited some new favourites, with Muti being one of the best.

But most importantly, it was nice to see that Vic has found her people. She has a nice group of friends she seems genuinely happy with and that makes me happy too. It takes a lot of time to meet people that fit with you, but when you do it gives you a lot of validation.
